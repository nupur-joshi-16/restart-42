UNIT 3
I am doing (present continuous)

Section A

1. I'm eating.
2. She's eating.
3. She isn't reading.
4. It's raining.
5. The sun isn't shining.
6. We're running.
7. They're running.
8. They aren't walking.

The present continuous is:
am/is/are + doing/eating/running/writing etc.


Section B

am/is/are + ing = something is happening now.
I'm working.
She's wearing a hat.
They're playing football.
I'm not watching television.
